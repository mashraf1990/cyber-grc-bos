PCI DSS Financial Institution, Saudi Arabia

PCI DSS Compliance Program

Challenge

A major financial institution required full PCI DSS compliance across all 12 requirements with an upcoming audit deadline.

Approach

Led end-to-end compliance program including gap analysis, remediation planning, evidence collection, and QSA audit coordination.

Outcome

Achieved audit readiness with a structured compliance program and clear evidence documentation.

SOC 2 SaaS Organization, USA

SOC 2 Readiness Assessment

Challenge

A US-based SaaS company needed SOC 2 Type II readiness to satisfy enterprise client requirements.

Approach

Conducted end-to-end readiness assessment, Trust Service Criteria mapping, and developed a compliance roadmap.

Outcome

Established a clear audit path with defined controls, evidence processes, and remediation plan.

Architecture Government Enterprise, Saudi Arabia

Security Architecture Review

Challenge

Approach

Outcome

Delivered prioritized recommendations for strengthening the project's security posture.
