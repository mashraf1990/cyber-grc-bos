## INDIVIDUAL SERVICE PAGES (modular)

### PCI DSS Compliancce
**Headline:** Full lifecycle compliance, from gap assessment to audit readiness.
**What we do:** We help organizations navigate the complexity of PCI DSS 4.0.1, covering all 12 requirements, 282 controls, and reporting procedures.
**Our approach:** Assess current state, plan remediation, execute evidence collection / control implementation, validate, prepare for QSA.
**Delvierables:** Gap repotr, control matrix, policy doc, evidence repository, readiness assessment, reommmendation list.
**Ideal for:** Banks, FinTech, e-commerce, any org accepting/processing cardholder data.

### ISO 27001
**Headline:** Build a compliant, certifiable ISMS.
**What we do:** Develop and implement ISO 27001:2022 aligned Information Security Management Systems from scratch or from current state.
**Approach:** Clauses 4-10, Annex A controls, risk assessment and treatment, statement of applicability.
**Deliverable:** ISMS documentation suite, implementation roadmap, internal audit, certification support.
**Ideal for:** SaaS companies, financial services, large enterprises.

### SOC 2
**Headline:** Achieve your SOC 2 report with confidence.
**What we do:** For tech companies requiring SOC 2, we lead them through readiness and compliance.
**Approach:** TSC mapping, evidence collection, control testing.
**Deliverble:** Readiness assessment, gap report, evidence dossier, test results, remediation status.
**Ideal for:** SaaS providers, cloud services, data centers.

### Risk Assessment

### Case Studies
**CASE STUDY 1: PCI DSS Saudi National Bank**
Business Challenge: SNB needed to maintain PCI DSS compliance while scaling operations, adding systems, and managing a complex environment.
Objectives:
- Maintaic current compliance level
- Reduce non-compliance gaps
- Streamline evidence collection
- Ensure audit readiness
Approach:
- Conducted comprehensive gap assessment.
- Developed a PCI DSS compliance program covering all 12 requirements.
- Implemented evidence gathering processes, conducted walkthroughs, and supported testinf.
- Provided progress reporting to management.
Methodology:
GRCA framework applied to PCI DSS controls. All 282 control statements mapped.
Results:
- 60% reduction in compliance gaps.
- Successful external audit readiness.
- Improved control documentation.
- Team awareness and competence increased.
Lessons: Engaging team early and establishing a continuous monitoring program are critical.

### CASE STUDY 2: Enterprise Security Arch Review (Diriyah)
Business Challenge: The national-level project required a comprehensive security review before further expansion.
Objectives:
- Identify security vulnerabilities
- Provide actionable recommendations
- Align to leading practice standards
Approach:
- Performed architecture discovery and contextual analysis.
- Reviewed network segmentation, firewalls, cloud security, application code, identity and access, logging, and physical security.
Methodology:
Used NIST SP 800-53 and CIS controls. Leveraged threat modeling (STRIDE) for identifying architectural weaknesses.
Deliverables:
- Report with technical findings
- Risk-scored vulnerabilities
- Prioritized remediation plan
Business Value: Better security posture, regulatory compliance, investor and client confidence.
Results: 30+ findings, 75% critical issues fixed in first phase, improved security awareness among team.
Lessons: Regular architecture review should be part of operational cadence.

### CASE STUDY 3: SOC 2 Readiness — NVITS (USA)
Business Challenge: Prepare for SOC 2 examination without prior experience.
Objectives:
- Identify gaps in controls
- Build SOC 2 evidence process
- Educate teams on SOC 2 requirements
Approach:
- Five-phase SOC 2 assignment covering all TSC.
- Interviews, document review, evidence gathering analysis.
- Recommendations tailored for the US market regulatory environment.
Deliverables: Gap analysis report, evidence list, control mapping, periodic status update.
Results:  100% of TSC assessed, actionable gaps identified, organizational alignment achieved.
Lessons: The initial TSC discovery had some complexity, requiring iterative clarification of requirements.

### CASE STUDY 4: SOC 2 Compliance — Parent.app
Business Challenge: Achieve SOC 2 compliance for a fast-moving app platform with limited resources.
Strategy: Tailor controls to business reality, prioritize high-impact activities, same as TSC but with startup pragmatism.
Deliverables: Policy documents, evidence collection scripts, status reporting.
Results: Effective compliance program, heightened awareness, reduced Turnaround time for external audit.
Lessons: For startups, integration of compliance into existing workflows is critical.

### CASE STUDY 5: Healthcare SaaS Security Review
Business Challenge: Healthcare platform needed cybersecurity assessment for SOC 2 and HIPAA consideration.
Approach: Security review of application, infrastructure, access controls, data protection, and logging.
Results: 20+ findings, compliance roadmap, improved security development practices.
Lessons: Early security engagement reduces overall cost of remediation.

### CASE STUDY 6: SAMA CSF Implementation
Business Challenge: A Saudi-based insurance company needed alignment with the Saudi Arabian Monetary Authority (SAMA) CSF for licensing and operational requirements.
Approach: Mapped current controls to SAMA CSF. Developed gap, identified required controls, implemented necessary changes, provided training.
Results: Achieve compliance, passed regulatory review, improved risk management maturity.
Lessons: A structured community of practice and training helped accelerate adoption.
