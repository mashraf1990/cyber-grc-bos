## LOGO CONCEPT
A modern wordmark logotype with a custom icon.

### Primary Logo Concept
**Icon:** An abstract hexagon shield with a checkmark inside, symbolizing governance and protection.
The hexagon represents the 6 sides of a comprehensive GRC program (Policy, Risk, Compliance, Technology, People, Process).
The checkmark signifies validation, approval, and audit success.

**Wordmark:** Using a bold, geometric sans-serif typeface (e.g., Montserrat Bold or Poppins Bold) with tracking set to 100. "MOHAMED ASHRAF" on the first line, "ABD EL-AZIZ" as the second line.

**Subline:** "Cybersecurity & GRC Consultant" or simply "G.R.C." in light weight beneath.

### Logo Variations to develop:
1. Full horizontal lockup (icon + wordmark)
2. Vertical lockup (icon above wordmark)
3. Icon only (favicon / avatar)
4. Wordmark only (for letterheads)
