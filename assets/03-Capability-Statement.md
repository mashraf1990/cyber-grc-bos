## PAGE 6 — METHODOLOGY

**The GRCA Framework**

| Phase | Activity | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Assess | Discovery, data collection, interviews, control self-assessment | 1-2 weeks |
| 2. Plan | Strategy, gap analysis, risk assessment, roadmap | 1-2 weeks |
| 3. Build | Policy, technical controls, training, evidence collection | 4-8 weeks |
| 4. Operate | Implementation support, monitoring, reporting | 4-12 weeks |
| 5. Audit | Internal audit, remediation, readiness | 2-4 weeks |
| 6. Sustain | Continuous improvement, quarterly reviews | Ongoing |
