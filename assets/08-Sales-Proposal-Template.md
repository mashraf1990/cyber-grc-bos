## 5. Approach & Methodology

Our approach follows the GRCA framework tailored for this engagement:

**Phase 1 — Assessment (Week 1-2)**
- Document review: existing policies, procedures, previous audit reports
- Stakeholder interviews
- Control testing and walkthroughs
- Gap analysis report

**Phase 2 — Planning (Week 2-3)**
- Risk assessment
- Prioritization of findings
- Roadmap and strategy
- Resource estimation

**Phase 3 — Implementation (Weeks 4-10)**
- Policy / procedure development
- Control implementation/improvement
- Evidence collection
- Progress reporting (weekly/bi-weekly)

**Phase 4 — Validation (Weeks 11-12)**
- Internal audit / pre-audit testing
- Remediation of identified issues
- Final review and readiness confirmation

**Phase 5 — Post-Implementation (Months 3-6)**
- Monitoring and adjustments
- Knowledge transfer and training
- Handover documentation
